About Khana Khazana

Built around traditional flavors and a more polished restaurant experience.

Khana Khazana started with a simple goal: serve Pakistani favorites with real depth, careful cooking, and a setting that feels welcoming from lunch to late dinner.

Our Story

What began as a passion for biryani, karahi, and charcoal-grilled staples became a menu shaped around comfort, generosity, and dishes people actually come back for.

We combine classic recipes, steady kitchen discipline, and fresh ingredients so every plate feels familiar but still restaurant-worthy.

Fresh Ingredients

Expert Kitchen

Traditional Taste
