How do I log in to KIU LMS?

Visit the Login page and enter your university username and password provided by the IT department.

How can I register for courses?

After logging in, go to Active Courses and select your semester courses during the registration period.

Where can I submit assignments?

Navigate to your course dashboard and open the Assignments section to upload your work before the deadline.

How do I check my grades?

Grades are available in real time under the Grades section of your student dashboard after faculty publishes them.

Who do I contact for technical support?

Email info@kiu.edu.pk or visit the Contact page to reach the LMS support team.
